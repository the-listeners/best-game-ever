A Game By The Listeners
  • About the Developers

Extraterrestrials have launched a missile strike in an attempt to clear solar space for their new time share resort. Unfortunately, this comes at the expense of Planet Kahn-Baan and the Kahn-Baanians. To save the planet and its residents a hero must rise up. Realize your destiny and become that hero!
How to Play:

To begin the user must enter a username, select a language, and then click the ‘Let’s Play’ button. This will direct the user to the game page where the stage will be set.

You will be presented with 5 missiles that must be eliminated before they strike the planet. Click the specific missile that you would like to target and correctly type out the english translation of the vocabulary word presented.

After the game has concluded see where you rank amongst the legions of heroes and try again!
